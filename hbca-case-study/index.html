HoneyBaked Ham Email Marketing

HoneyBaked Ham is a national food brand that owns hundreds of retail locations and numerous online catalogs for product shipment across the country. As part of Thrive, I've managed the entirity of HoneyBaked California's email marketing for 3 years. HoneyBaked California sends, on average, 18 email campaigns during the holiday seasons. I oversee these campaigns from conception, copywriting, design, development, through send and data analysis. The data analysis has become one of my favorite tasks, since it allows me to learn what worked, what didn't, and then apply that knowledge. Along with the data analysis, I began to heavily experiment with A/B testing and specifically sentiment in copy and graphics to enhance open and click through rates for each campaign.
