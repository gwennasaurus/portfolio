More About Gwen

Hey there, I'm Gwen! I am a twenty-something residing in the great American city of Boston. I began my journey in tech as a 12 year old girl interested in crafting pretty pixels and translating them with code to the web. Many moons later, I found myself with an extreme passion for design that has transcended years. This love gave me my career. However, work never feels like work to me; it's just an extension of myself. I am happiest when I am creating something, anything; it's what I was born to do. This extends into my personal life, as well.

I am an avid "DIYer." I love learning and getting my hands dirty. This comes in handy not only in design, but in every walk of life. I also live for experiences, and enjoy the simple pleasures gained from exploring. You can find me beboping around Boston with a moto jacket, metallic sneakers, and my camera always out. I even have a blog dedicated to my recent move and adventures in the city: Gwen Exploring Boston. It's been an amazing life thus far, and I look forward to a very bright and prosperous future.

...OH, and I have a cat named Pixel.
